CAMRU Discord Server

Join the Camru Discord Server for BCIS Students! Welcome to the Camru Discord Server, a digital hub for BCIS students at Mount Royal University! This platform is your go-to place for everything related to your BCIS program. From study groups to tech discussions and networking.
Join the discord server.

Server Features:

  1. Career Tips and Opportunities: Channels for job postings, internship opportunities, and career advice.
  2. Course-Specific Channels: Dedicated channels for each BCIS course where you can discuss assignments, share resources, and collaborate on projects.
  3. Voice Chat Rooms: Join live study sessions or just hang out with your peers.
  4. Industry News: Stay updated with the latest in the tech world.
